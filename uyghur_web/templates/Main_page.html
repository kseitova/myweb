About us

Welcome to the Uyghur Sacramento online community. This website serves as a place where people can find out about upcoming Uyghur events and gatherings in Sacramento. We welcome anyone who wants to learn about our culture, traditions, and history. We hope to build a strong and friendly community of Uyghurs who live in Sacramento, San Francisco, and any surrounding areas. Any questions/suggestions can be directed to the website administrator at uyghursacramento@mail.com
